PET HEALTH ADVISOR

Canine Parvovirus

Symptoms

  • Severe vomiting
  • Loss of appetite
  • Fever
  • Bloody diarrhea
  • Lethargy and weakness
  • Dehydration
  • Abdominal pain

Medication

  • Hospitalization for intravenous (IV) fluids to prevent dehydration
  • Antiemetic drugs to control vomiting
  • Antibiotics to prevent secondary bacterial infections
  • Nutritional support, often through a feeding tube if necessary
  • Medications to reduce fever and abdominal pain
  • Close monitoring by a veterinarian
